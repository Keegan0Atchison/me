I am finishing my degree in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at NYU (Spring 2026). In my available time, I work on projects involving robotic arms, mycelium textiles, and democratizing technologies.

Currently, I work as an R&D engineer at Terreform ONE, where I develop material and mechanical systems for commissioned products (Estée Lauder Companies). Previously, I created a co-op program between NYU and WASP 3D (Massa Lombarda, Italy), where I led material development projects for TU Eindhoven and the Italian Space Agency. I have also worked at CarbonQuest and the Taylor NYU Lab to build software systems for emerging technologies.

For those who’d like to speak more about my work or the ideas discussed here, please reach out. I am interested in what piles of thought do for people, and what I can learn and abstract from them.
